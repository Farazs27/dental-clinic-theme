SMILE MAKEOVER

Your path to a perfect smile

On this page we introduce you to all the steps involved in a smile makeover. Every step is carefully designed to ensure we can deliver what you envision. Our goal is to get to know you and your personality before we begin any restorative work.

Initial consultations, photography, digital smile design, diagnostic mock-ups and trial setups with various temporary restorations are just some of the many steps we take to ensure we can give you a smile you truly love.

In our process, patients are an integral part of the team — no great work can be done without close collaboration and mutual understanding.

O U R   W O R K F L O W

1

F I R S T   C O N S U L T   &   D O C U M E N T A T I O N

Our process begins with an introduction between you and our team. This opens a conversation to understand your wishes and desires. Here, complete documentation and photos are taken so we can analyze your face, smile and personality.

2

S T U D Y   &   D E S I G N

We use all obtained documentation to carefully study and design the case. Often we start our smile makeover process with an (ortho) restorative mock-up to share our vision with you before any treatment is performed.

3

P R E P A R A T I O N   B A S E D   O N
D I A G N O S T I C   W A X - U P

Once our final design is confirmed by the team and by you, we perform minimally invasive preparation of the teeth via an additive mock-up. This allows us to remove only what is necessary for the final ceramics. Our philosophy revolves around tooth preservation. Less is more.

4

T E M P O R A R Y   R E S T O R A T I O N S

The temporary phase is one of the most important stages of any smile makeover for our team. This is an opportunity for you to wear and live with the design we have in mind. Here our goal is to open a conversation for any changes and achieve what truly makes you happy before we begin fabricating the ceramic restorations.

5

F I N A L   P L A C E M E N T   O F
C E R A M I C   R E S T O R A T I O N S

We have a very thorough process for the placement of the final ceramic restorations. This step is performed under rubber dam isolation (unless contraindicated) to achieve maximum bond strength and create a clean environment for the bonding steps. Most of our dental procedures are performed under rubber dam isolation — the benefits are numerous. Ask us why during your next visit!

6

F I N A L   R E S U L T   &   D O C U M E N T A T I O N

After the placement of the final restorations, we perform multiple tissue checks to ensure the health of the gums. These appointments are accompanied by final photos of our work — the most satisfying part of this process.
