# Team Photos

Add your team member photos to this folder.

## Required Images

Replace or add these images:

### Dentists
- `dentist-1.jpg` - Lead dentist photo (recommended: 600x800px or similar portrait ratio)
- `dentist-2.jpg` - Second dentist photo (optional)

### Hygienists  
- `hygienist-1.jpg` - Dental hygienist photo

### Assistants
- `assistant-1.jpg` - First dental assistant
- `assistant-2.jpg` - Second dental assistant (optional)

### Background
- `page-hero-bg.jpg` - Hero background image for pages (recommended: 1920x600px)

## Photo Guidelines

1. **Professional Quality**: Use high-quality, well-lit professional photos
2. **Consistent Style**: Try to maintain consistent lighting, background, and framing
3. **Format**: JPG or PNG format
4. **Size**: Recommended minimum 600px width for team photos
5. **Background**: Neutral or clinic-related backgrounds work best

## Stock Photo Resources

If you need stock photos, you can find professional dental/medical images at:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/dentist) (free)
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com/search/dentist/) (free)
- [Shutterstock](https://shutterstock.com) (paid)
- [Adobe Stock](https://stock.adobe.com) (paid)

## Tips

- Smiling, approachable photos work best for dental clinics
- Wearing professional attire (white coat, scrubs) adds credibility
- Eye contact with the camera creates connection with visitors
