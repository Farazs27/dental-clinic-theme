FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I make an appointment?

You can make an appointment in several ways: by phone at +1 (555) 123-4567, by email at info@yourdentalclinic.com, or through the online registration form on our website.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID and insurance card. If you have a referral from another dentist, please bring that as well. For emergencies, it's helpful to have a list of any medications you're taking.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers. Please contact your insurer or ask us when making your appointment to confirm coverage.

Can I come for emergency care?

Yes, for acute tooth pain or dental emergencies, we try to help you within 24 hours. Call us directly for emergency care: +1 (555) 123-4567.

Is parking available?

Yes, free parking is available directly in front of our clinic. You don't need to worry about parking costs.

Do you offer evening appointments?

Currently we are open Monday through Friday (except Wednesday) from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. For special cases, we can try to schedule an appointment outside these hours.
